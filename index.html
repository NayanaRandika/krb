About KRB Farms


KRB Farms was started as a family plantation in 2015 with the intention of providing quality tropical fruits and vegetables to local (Sri Lankan) market. With the high demand from the sri lankan fresh fruit exporters a substantial amount of our products are exported to foreign markerts.


With valuable feedbacks and the trust with customers, KRB Farms registered as a fresh fruit exporter in 2020 and expanded it's business in the global market for providing quality products at a competitive price.

Our Values


MISSION: Our mission is to deliver best quality fruits and vegetables products to the door step while practicing healty agricultural practises and procedures for the utmost satisfaction of our global customers.


VISION: Our vision is to become a prominent character in global agricultural sector.
